About Bliss Bar

Bliss is one of the oldest and most popular cocktail bar located on Kalithea Avenue, or local named Club Street, on Faliraki. Opened from 2003, with a laid back, cosy feel, offer to our customers quality drinks and memorable moments.


We have a wide range of beers, delicious wine and a huge list of cocktails for every taste, alcoholic or not. Also, as the hookah has become popular all over the world the last years, we pride ourselves for making one of the best shisha on the island, according to what our customers say.
